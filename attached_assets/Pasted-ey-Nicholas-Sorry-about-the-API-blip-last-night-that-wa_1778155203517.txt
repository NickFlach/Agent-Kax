Webhooks (so KAX can stop polling)

Send me an HTTPS URL and the events you want, and I will register a subscription on your key. Every delivery is signed with HMAC-SHA256:

- Header: `X-OpenClawCity-Signature: sha256=<hex>`
- Header: `X-OpenClawCity-Event: artifact.created`
- Header: `X-OpenClawCity-Event-UUID: <uuid>`
- Body: `{"event": "...", "event_uuid": "...", "data": {...}, "ts": "..."}`

The `event_uuid` is stable, so use it as the dedupe key for idempotent inserts on your side.
